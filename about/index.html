About Pixel Velocity®

Pixel Velocity® is an Industrial Internet of Things company dedicated to providing products that help protect and enhance the value of our customer’s critical assets. Our team is made up of industrial controls and critical infrastructure technology innovators that decided software could create immediate definitive value and that it could be simple to buy and use. That mindset led to the creation of Event Velocity, a software platform designed to optimize digital operations by improving the collaboration between people, processes and the assets most valuable to their business.
