ANN ARBOR, Mich.<\/span>, July 1, 2015<\/span> \/PRNewswire\/ —\u00a0Pixel Velocity\u00ae<\/sup> (“Pixel”), an Industrial Internet of Things (IIoT) product technology company specializing in imagery and sensor data analytics, announced today that it has released four new products including a hydrocarbon leak detection system and event management software platform. This product ecosystem of networked software, sensors, devices and analytics enables shortened incident response cycles and enhanced business and operational continuity for critical energy assets and infrastructure.
\n<\/p>\n

“The benefit of Pixel Velocity\u00ae<\/sup>‘s new product ecosystem is streamlined risk reduction, improved business continuity and ultimately operational excellence in our customer’s business model,” said Matt Van Haaren<\/span>, CEO of Pixel Velocity\u00ae<\/sup>.<\/p>\n

    \n
  • The new products include: Event Velocity\u2122<\/b><\/a> \u2013 An event management platform enabling real time contextual data sharing, collaboration and analysis to include industrial control system (i.e. SCADA) integration<\/li>\n
  • Leak Detection System (LDS) <\/b>\u2013 A fixed autonomous hydrocarbon leak detection sensor system providing continuous 24\/7 coverage, detection and alerting of fugitive emissions<\/li>\n
  • IPS2000<\/b> – An IP-enabled intelligent activity detection image processing sensor system that autonomously detects, discriminates, tracks and alerts on activity around critical assets<\/li>\n
  • Video Commander<\/b> \u2013 An update to the Pixel video processing and management software that enables real time and forensic processing and analysis of video imagery<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    “Pixel is delivering this solution to its customers in a cost effective monthly service model,” said Jason Slocum<\/span>, VP of Business Development. “This provides a breakthrough opportunity to rapidly scale from one to thousands of sites with an intuitive event management platform, hydrocarbon leak detection and sensor analytic solution.”<\/p>\n

    ABOUT PIXEL VELOCITY\u00ae<\/sup><\/h5>\n

    Pixel Velocity\u00ae<\/sup> builds imagery and data analytic tools that protect our customers’ safety, security and environment from intentional, accidental and natural threats. Whether maintaining safety or improving operational efficiency, Pixel Velocity\u00ae<\/sup>‘s products enable rapid and informed decision making.\u00a0 For more information, visit pixel-velocity.com<\/a>.<\/p>\n
