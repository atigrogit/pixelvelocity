Ann Arbor, May 7, 2019\u2014Pixel Velocity is pleased to announce that it has added talented executives in sales, marketing, engineering, and finance to support market demand for its Event Velocity\u00ae software. Event Velocity is a digital operations software platform for automating the monitoring and management of remote and hazardous assets.<\/p>\n

\u201cAttracting a group this talented really shows how people view the market opportunity for Event Velocity software,\u201d commented Pixel Velocity CEO Jonathan Murray.<\/p>\n

Patrick Talley has joined as Executive Vice President of Sales. Patrick brings significant prior experience in software sales and entrepreneurship. Over a distinguished 25-year career in oil and gas and healthcare, Patrick has closed over $250 million in enterprise sales. He is responsible for sales to the energy and utilities markets.<\/p>\n

In Marketing, Pixel is pleased to welcome Bob Chunn, who has led marketing and advertising for many corporate and entrepreneurial ventures. Bob has specific expertise in digital and content marketing and rose to the position of Vice President of Digital, at Borders Group. He will focus on strategic marketing, sales and customer satisfaction.<\/p>\n

Isaac Shi, co-founder of Houston-based Golden Section Technology (GST), has joined Pixel as Chief Technology Officer. Isaac has developed multiple enterprise software applications and mobile and big data solutions for millions of end users. He also has co-founded SaaS software ventures in health informatics and industrial automation.<\/p>\n

Matt Cassell rounds out the new additions to the team, focusing on finance. Matt has over 20 years of corporate finance experience, including leading the finance function\u00a0for\u00a0an education-technology startup in Chicago.\u00a0 He returned to Michigan in 2013 to lead strategy and analysis for the mortgage division of MB Financial Bank.\u00a0He has worked for several entrepreneurial ventures over the course of his career.<\/p>\n

With these additions, Pixel Velocity is positioned to serve an expanding customer base and to continue to innovate it leading digital oilfield solutions. \u201cEvent Velocity enables customers to remotely monitor all of their assets 24\/7, including their most remote and hazardous sites. Every day we identify a new opportunity to simplify, streamline or enhance customers operations,\u201d said Mr. Murray. \u201cOur newest team members bring decades of experience in scaling technology companies, which will help us to expand our market presence.\u201d<\/p>\n

ABOUT PIXEL VELOCITY\u00ae<\/sup><\/h2>\n

Pixel Velocity Incorporated, is an Ann Arbor-based software company that develops IIoT edge computing solutions. The Company\u2019s Event Velocity software is a unified environment to consolidate, analyze, display, and alert data from any IIoT sensor. It includes video analytic capabilities for motion detection, infrared analytics for hydrocarbon leak detection, and event management. Pixel Velocity partners with Dell Technologies IoT Solutions Partner Program<\/a>, Unicom, Erasco Group, and Twin Eagle Consulting to serve the upstream, midstream, and downstream segments of the oil and gas industry and the utilities market.<\/p>\n
